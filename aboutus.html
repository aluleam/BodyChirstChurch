About Us

Our Mission

We are committed to spreading the Gospel of Christ, fostering spiritual growth, and serving our community with love and compassion.

Our History

The Body of Christ Church was founded in 2012 by Senior Pastor Mikumba Alimase and his wife, Andjelani Makano. The church started with just two members and has grown to a thriving congregation of over 250 members. It welcomes people from all walks of life and backgrounds to be part of a vibrant and supportive community of faith.

Our services are held every Sunday from 1:00 PM to 4:00 PM. In case of changes to the time or location due to conferences or other events, updates will be provided here. The church has grown significantly over the past decade, helping individuals deepen their faith and develop spiritually.
